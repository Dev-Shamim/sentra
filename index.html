The Mission Behind Sentra

We started with a simple realization: Expert mentorship shouldn't be a luxury. Every student deserves guidance to realize their full potential and dreams.

Many professionals have years of invaluable experience — in schools, in admissions offices, or as specialized tutors — yet connecting with students who need them most has traditionally been limited by geography and cost.

Sentra changes that. By creating a marketplace connecting students directly with counselors, we extend the reach of expert guidance and make it accessible to everyone.
